CHEMICAL PROPERTIES OF DRILLING MUD PROPERTIES

  • Clay weight (density)
    It is measured in units of pounds / gallons, gm / cm 3, pounds / foot 3, as well as pounds / inch 1000 feet (pressure gradient). The device that measures density is called a mud balance (MUD BALANCE).
  • The apparent viscosity (MARSH FUNNEL VISCOSITY), which is the time in seconds required to fill a volume of (946 cm 3) through the Marsh funnel, measured in seconds (SECOND).
    Note: The measured viscosity of water is 26 sec.
  • FILTRATION LOSSES & MUD KAKE
    It is meant by the amount of water (cm3) filtered through a porous paper (FILTER PAPER) after applying pressure on a clay sample of (100) Pa/Ing 2 in a period of (30) minutes. The examination is carried out by means of the filter press, as well as by measuring the thickness of the mud cake in millimeters. Note: The filter is measured within (7.5) minutes, and the result is multiplied by (2).
  • FBasal PH
    It is measured using paper that changes color according to (PH). There is also an electric device.
    Note:
    1. Water is a neutral solution (i.e. not bottomless or acidic) and its PH is equal to (7), as for basic solutions it is between (8-14) and for acidic solutions it is between 1-6
    2. The mud is alkaline because all the chemicals used do not undergo a chemical reaction unless the surroundings are alkaline. Therefore, before starting the threading operations, caustic soda is added to make the surroundings alkaline.
